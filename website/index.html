About GriffinCoin

The infrastructure around superannuation has been relatively unchanged for decades, the banks which host these superannuation funds are rarely known for transparency or performing in the best interest of the general public. Griffincoin aims to add transparency to these systems and decrease risk through the implementation of a blockchain to introduce trustless transactions and smart contracts.

Australian citizens need to have trust in their financial future. Smart contracts ensure that the Government will pay citizens back their money and interest, making smart contracts perfect for Superannuation agreements. This is due to the immutability of the contracts; they cannot be removed or cheated. Griffincoin allows for Australians to fully trust in their financial agreements with the wealthy giants of the world, creating a sence of freedom and security in the way their money is invested.
